The Official Bourbon Trail Distilleries

Shelby Browning-University of Kentucky Geography Student

Kentucky is famous for a few things, most important of which (in my mind at least) is Kentucky Bourbon. With almost 10 Million aging barrels of bourbon and other distilled spirits, it's no surprise that the Commonwealth of Kentucky would like to capitalize on this importance. One of the major tourist activities to do while in Kentucky is The Bourbon Trail. This trail has 18 officially recognized distilleries that are a part of the Kentucky Distiller's Association (KDA). While only 18 distilleries are formally recognized as stops on the Bourbon Trail, plenty more craft distilleries exist in many locations all across the state.

Tools used in this project

  • Data is shown on Mapbox web map with geolocation services enabled.
  • A dataset of distillery point locations was produced in Mapbox with the use of information from https://kybourbontrail.com/distilleries/
